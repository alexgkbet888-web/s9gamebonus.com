Quick answer

This guide explains what users should check when a welcome bonus or promo claim is not visible after registration.

Checklist

  1. Check the bonus eligibility and expiry first.
  2. Confirm whether a deposit or turnover rule is required.
  3. Avoid pages that promise guaranteed bonus release.
  4. Prepare account time and masked screenshots for support.
